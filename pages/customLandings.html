This mock up site was created to show you how the flow of content can affect a website. It is a very simplistic website that takes advatage of how the content is laid out. The first thing to take note of is the main content, it funnels down like a upside down pyramid. Layours like this fill up the page & is pleasing on the visitors eyes without needing a complex layout. Notice how much blank space there is, but because of the symetry of the content and how it's organized it's overlooked. Also pay attention to the site colors. It starts with a dark blue and ends with the dark blue seperating the main content from the header and footer. Also, the nice green color in the middle of the site content, notice how it compliments the blue, and gives your helps to mentally fill up space and keep the content organized.
